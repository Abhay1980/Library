Welcome to Our Library

Your gateway to knowledge and adventure.

A library is the delivery room for the birth of ideas, a place where history comes to life. The Study Mart is a Thakudawara based library facility which aims to take you one step closer to success. The key factors that define "The Study Mart" are comfortable seating, good lighting, a peaceful environment, cooperative staff and a good internet connection and we think that all these factors are important for your study. More than that, we also provide cafeteria facility for your breaks, which will help you to re-energize and refocus yourself. The library remains open 24*7 for your convenience and it is situated at a very convenient and safe place. With all these facilities under one roof, we believe that you will be able to increase your study hours and make yourself more productive.
